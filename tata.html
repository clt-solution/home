About Sherwin

Sherwin is a highly skilled Network Engineer specializing in designing, deploying, and managing secure, high-performance network infrastructures using high-end, enterprise-grade equipment with military-grade security protocols. Experienced in configuring and operating advanced networking hardware from MikroTik, Cisco (Nexus and Catalyst series), and Juniper, Sherwin ensures resilient and secure connectivity across diverse environments, including large-scale corporate networks such as Mia Tech Internet and Cable Installation Services and Power Tech Line, Roxas City.
